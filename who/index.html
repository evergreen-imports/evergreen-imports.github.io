Who are we?

要日本語テキスト
Evergreen Imports was created for the sole purpose of bringing fresh and delicious Northwest-style ales from Washington to Japan. We believe that fresher is better, and no other state has fresher beer than Washington because of its access to local ingredients and highly skilled brewers.

Seattle is one of the closest ports to Japan from North America, and ocean freight spends less time at sea from there. With our small family owned brewery suppliers filling kegs often within hours of delivery to the port, no other importer to Japan can have their beer arrive fresher and faster. Our refrigerated containers and the cool ocean waters of the northern Pacific Ocean ensure our beer stays chilled throughout the voyage north, skirting along the Alaskan coast before crossing near the Aleutian Islands before reaching Japan.

Locally, our air-conditioned warehouse and cool-truck shipping keeps the beer in cold and stable conditions, making sure your beer reaches you in the best possible condition outside of the brewery’s own taproom.

Our mission is to bring truly local Washington brews to pubs and restaurants in Japan. Some selections we offer from smaller breweries are not normally found outside the brewery’s own taproom, and are imported in extremely limited quantities, sometimes one keg at a time. Other beers are carefully selected in order to provide a range of styles and flavors, and every beer we import can stand on its own with or without other offerings. There are no ‘filler’ beers in our catalog, and each beer we import is our carefully considered choice for serving the Japanese beer market. Pick any beer in this catalog, and you will not be disappointed or we will gladly replace it.

Evergreen is committed to bringing you the award winning, local taste of Washington beer to Japan. Cheers!
