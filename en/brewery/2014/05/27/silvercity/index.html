Silver City Brewery

In 1996, brothers Scott & Steve Houmes shared a vision of opening a world class brewpub and restaurant. The brothers teamed up with local Thomas Kemper brewer & founder of the West Sound Brewers Club, brewmaster “Big Daddy” Don Spencer, and Silver City was born.
Throughout the years Big Daddy’s brews have won nearly 60 awards in the prestigious World Beer Cup, North American Beer Awards, & the Great American Beer Festival, making Silver City one of the most decorated breweries in the Northwest.
Over time, demand for Silver City beers outside the restaurant began to grow. Scott & Steve decided to make the move to begin meeting that demand in late 2008. Quickly, Silver City beers became available all around the Puget Sound and with this success the brewers quickly ran out of room & capacity at the Silverdale flagship brewpub.
In May of 2010 Silver City opened its new production facility in Bremerton, WA and ceased brewing operations in Silverdale. The facility allowed Silver City the room to grow with demand. Silver City is now more widely available in bars and restaurants in the Pacific Northwest, and now Japan!
