Two Beers Brewing Co.

In 2007, Two Beers Brewing Head Brewer Joel VandenBrink turned his hobby into his passion. After several years on homebrewing in his kitchen – with nothing more than a five-gallon stockpot – Joel took a leap of faith. In five years, Two Beers Brewing has more than quadrupled its size, with sales nearing 4,000 barrels in 2012. From its humble homebrew roots, Two Beers is now available at 300 bars and restaurants throughout Washington.
